### Calendar
**Usage:**
> To get current month's Calendar according to your timezone.

**Trigger**
> Trigger Type: `Command` or `Regex`
> 
> Trigger: `Calendar` or `\A(?:\-|<@!?204255221017214977>)\s*(?:date|calendar|day|time)(?: +|\z)`
